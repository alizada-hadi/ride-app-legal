# Driver Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

## Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Harja Driver App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our driver platform and transportation services.

By using the Harja Driver App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

# Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the Driver App:

## Personal Information

* Full name
* Phone number
* Email address
* Profile photo (If provided)
* National identification information (Tazkira or Passport)
* Driver license information
* Vehicle registration information

## Location Information

We collect real-time location information to operate ride and transportation services, including:

* Current device location
* Background location during active driver sessions
* Pickup and drop-off locations
* Route and trip tracking

## Ride & Driver Activity Information

We may collect information related to your driver activity, including:

* Trip history
* Ride acceptance and cancellation records
* Driver ratings and reviews
* Earnings and transaction history
* Online/offline status
* Driving activity timestamps

## Device Information

We may automatically collect device-related information such as:

* Device type and model
* Operating system
* App version
* IP address
* Device identifiers
* Mobile network information

---

# How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

* To provide transportation services
* To connect drivers with passengers
* To calculate fares and earnings
* To verify driver identity and eligibility
* To improve app performance and safety
* To provide customer support
* To send ride and account notifications
* To prevent fraud and unauthorized activities
* To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

---

# Location Data

Location access is essential for the Driver App to function properly.

We use location information to:

* Match drivers with nearby passengers
* Enable navigation and routing
* Track trips in real time
* Improve ride safety and service quality
* Monitor active driver sessions

Location tracking may continue while the driver is online or during active rides, even if the app is running in the background.

* Account alerts
* Security notifications
